### Who are you, and what do you do?

I'm [Adam Newbold](https://adam.omg.lol "Adam's website,"), a web programmer living in [Louisville](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Louisville,_Kentucky "The Wikipedia entry for Louisville in Kentucky."). I run [omg.lol](https://home.omg.lol "A BBS-like website."), which someone once described as "a [BBS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bulletin_board_system "The Wikipedia entry for BBS.") adjusted for inflation," as well as [Neatnik LLC](https://www.neatnik.net/ "Adam's web development company."), where I work on internet miscellany.

### What do you use to get the job done?

Lots and lots of [PHP][], which I started learning nearly three decades ago and never stopped. I use the [FastCGI Process Manager][fpm] with [Caddy][] to bring my silly scripts to life in the browser. If I have to use a database, it's [SQLite][] or [MariaDB][], but I'd rather store JSON in a flatfile. For mail handling, I use [Postfix][] with [Postgrey][], [OpenDKIM][], [OpenDMARC][], and [OpenARC][]. For DNS, I use [BIND 9][bind]. All of this is powered by [Debian][] servers running on [Hetzner][] hardware.

For programming and writing, I use [Vim][] and [Nova][nova.2] interchangeably. [Boxy][boxy-svg] is my go-to tool for SVG and icon work, and [Pixelmator Pro][pixelmator-pro] for sticker design and other graphics work. I use [Glyphs][] to create and manage custom fonts (mostly icon fonts), and [Wakamai Fondue][wakamai-fondue] for debugging those. I store all of my notes (mostly server build and service configuration stuff) in [Obsidian][] and make heavy use of its wikilink syntax.

I have a small home office where I work, and it has just one computer: a 2022 [Mac Studio][mac-studio] which is still chugging along. That's what I use to do pretty much everything. I don't use laptops because I'm completely tied to my [Happy Hacking Keyboard][happy-hacking-keyboard] and [Logitech MX Master 3 Mouse][mx-master-3]. If I have to do some kind of emergency computery work when I'm away from my office, I use an [iPhone 16][iphone-16] with [Prompt 3][prompt-ios] to connect to stuff.

When away from screens, my main tools are [Uniball Signo 1mm Pens][signo-gel-impact] and Kokuyo Campus B5 Dotted Notebooks.

### What would be your dream setup?

Honestly? I would love to have a [Rotom Phone](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Rotom_Phone "A smartphone owned by a Rotom Pokémon.").
